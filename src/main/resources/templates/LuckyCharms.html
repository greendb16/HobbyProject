Lucky Charms

Ingredients

  • Lucky Charms Cereal
  • Milk

Directions

  1. Open box of cereal
  2. Pour cereal into bowl
  3. Eat with Spoon
Alternative:
  1. Open box of cereal
  2. Take Fistful of cereal
  3. Put cereal in mouth
Alternative:
  1. Open box of cereal
  2. Sort between marshmallows and non-marshmallows
  3. Throw away non-marshmallows
  4. Eat Marshmallows

Serves 1
