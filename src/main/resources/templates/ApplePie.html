Apple Pie

Ingredients

  • 5 Granny Smith Apples
  • 5 Golden Delicious Apples
  • Sugar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Butter
  • Cinnamon
  • Pie Crust
  • Lemon

Directions

  1. Peel and Slice Apples
  2. Zest Lemon
  3. Juice Lemon
  4. Combine Sugar, Brown Sugar, and Apples
  5. Cook Apples over medium ~15 min
  6. Cool Apples
  7. Drain Apples saving liquid
  8. Put Pie Crust in dish
  9. Put Apples in Pie Crust
  10. Combine Lemon juice and Apple juice
  11. Pour Juice mix over apples
  12. Apply top crust
  13. Apply Egg White to pie crust
  14. bake at 425 Degrees for 15 min
  15. Rotate pie 1801 Degrees, Continue Cooking at 375 Degrees for 20 min

Serves 8-10
