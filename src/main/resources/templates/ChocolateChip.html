Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Salt
  • Baking Soda

Directions

  1. Combine Butter and Sugar
  2. Beat in Eggs
  3. Add Vanilla
  4. Add Flour, Salt, and Baking Soda
  5. Add Chocolate Chips
  6. Bake for 10 min at 350 Degrees

Serves 12
