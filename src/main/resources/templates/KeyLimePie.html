Key Lime Pie

Ingredients

  • Limes
  • Condensed Milk
  • Eggs
  • GrahamCracker
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Juice and zest limes
  2. Whisk egg yolks and limes
  3. Whisk in Condensed milk
  4. Chill in fridge
  5. Crush Crackers
  6. Mix Crackers with butter
  7. Cook crust 15 min in pie dish
  8. Add cream to crust
  9. Cook pie 25 min
  10. Chill Pie

Serves 8-10
