Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Meat
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Parmasian Cheese

Directions

  1. Boil Lasagna Noodles
  2. Brown Meat
  3. Combine Meat and Sauce
  4. Layer in dish:
    • Meat-Sauce
    • Ricotta Cheese
    • Noodle
    • Parmasian Cheese
    • Repeat
  5. Cook 1 hr at 350 Degrees

Serves 8-10
